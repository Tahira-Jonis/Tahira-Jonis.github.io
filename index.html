About Me

My name is Tahira Jonis and I'm 22 years old. I'm a girl born and raised on the island of Curaçao. Designing is something that has fascinated me, but the possibilities of studying something like this in Curaçao were limited. Thats is why I decided to leave my home to come to the Netherlands.

Because I wanted to design apps and websites, I've decided to enroll for Communication & Multimedia Design. I have made websites in the past and also, I have experience with coding languages like JavaScript. I realized in the first year of studying CMD that my view of the study was narrow, but with time my interest in interaction design started to grow.

The process of creating a concept fascinates me. And in addition to that I am a very creative person and also like to work with other people, that's why I think that CMD suits me.
